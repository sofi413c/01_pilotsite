About the project

The project had to display a person with a passion for something. This video had to portrait someone for one minute while containing B-rolls, motion graphics and establishment shots. My person has a passion for cooking.

Tiago Neves

Tiago is 20 years old, born and raised in Portugal, Lisabon. He’s an exchange student and has been living in Copenhagen since February. Daily, Tiago is studying Data Management at IT University of Copenhagen.
His passion for cooking started when he was younger and his father started to cook complex dishes for him and his brother. For Tiago, cooking means time to relax and to not think about anything else.

Video

The purpose of the video is to highlight how cooking, in this case, can evoke nostalgia and bring people together across cultures.
